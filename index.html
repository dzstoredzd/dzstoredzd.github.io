A lost or broken phone shouldn't mean a lost business. With one tap you can sign in with Google and back up your data to a private folder in your own Google Drive, then restore it on a new device.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Still have a question? Reach us on WhatsApp or by email — we answer like humans, not tickets.

Do I need the internet to use Store Soft?

No. Store Soft is offline-first. Selling, stock, debt and reports all work with no connection. The internet is only needed if you choose to back up to Google Drive or download an update.

Is my shop data sent to any server?

No. Your business data stays in a local database on your own device. We do not run a server that collects it. The only time data leaves your device is if you turn on Google Drive backup, and then it goes only to your own private Drive folder.

What does the Google sign-in access?

Only a hidden app folder in your own Google Drive, using the limited drive.appdata permission. Store Soft cannot see or open any of your other Drive files, photos or documents. You can revoke access and delete the backups at any time from your Google account.

How much does it cost?

You can try it free. Unlocking is a one-time license for a single shop — no monthly subscription. Optional paid install, product import and migration are available. Contact us on WhatsApp for current pricing.

Which languages are supported?

Arabic (with full right-to-left layout) and French, with English on the way. You can switch language in the settings.
